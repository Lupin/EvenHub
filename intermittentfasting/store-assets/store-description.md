Track your fast.  Stay consistent — right on your G2 glasses.

Intermittent Fasting brings your fasting schedule onto your Even Realities G2 display. Glance at your glasses to see whether you're in your fasting or eating window, how much time remains, 
Two display modes:  

- Text mode  with countdown timer tucked in the upper corners
- Timeline mode with a full-width progress bar showing exactly where you are in your day. 

7 fasting presets: 
14:10 (Beginner), 16:8 (Classic), 18:6 (Intermediate), 20:4 (Advanced), OMAD (One Meal), 5:2 (2-Day), and ADF (Alternate). 

Custom start time: shift your fasting window while keeping the preset's duration. 

6 languages: English, Français, Español, 中文, 日本語, 한국어. 

- 12h or 24h time format 
- Dark / Light / System theme
