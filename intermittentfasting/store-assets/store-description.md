# Intermittent Fasting — Store Description

**Tagline:** Track your fast. See your progress. Stay consistent — right on your G2 glasses.

---

## About

Intermittent Fasting brings your fasting schedule onto your Even Realities G2 display. Glance at your glasses to see whether you're in your fasting or eating window, how much time remains, and your daily progress — no phone needed.

Two display modes: a clean **Text mode** with countdown timer tucked in the upper corners, and a **Timeline mode** with a full-width Unicode progress bar showing exactly where you are in your day. Double-tap to switch.

When you enter a new fasting or eating phase, the glasses briefly show a transition alert and your phone can notify you with a sound — so you never miss a window.

## Features

- **7 fasting presets** — 14:10 (Beginner), 16:8 (Leangains), 18:6 (Extended), 20:4 (Warrior), OMAD (One Meal A Day), 5:2 (Weekday), and Alternate Day Fasting (ADF)
- **Custom schedule** — set your own fasting hours
- **Two display modes** — Text (discreet corners with countdown) or Timeline (full-width progress bar)
- **Phase transition notifications** — visual alert on glasses + phone notification + sound when entering a new phase
- **6 languages** — English, Français, Español, 中文, 日本語, Português
- **12h or 24h time format** — with AM/PM support
- **Dark / Light / System theme** — companion app respects your phone settings
- **Fully offline** — no account, no cloud, no tracking. All data stays in localStorage on your device.

## Requirements

- Even Realities G2 glasses
- Even Realities App v2.0.0 or later
- Even Hub SDK 0.0.10+

## Privacy

Intermittent Fasting stores all data locally using your device's localStorage. No data is ever transmitted to external servers. No analytics. No tracking. No account required.
