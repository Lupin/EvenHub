# Privacy Policy — Intermittent Fasting

**Last updated:** June 10, 2026

## Summary

Intermittent Fasting does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All information stays on your device.

## Data Storage

The application stores the following data exclusively in your device's local storage (localStorage):

- Your selected fasting preset (e.g., 16:8, OMAD)
- Your custom fasting schedule (start and end times)
- Your display mode preference (text or timeline)
- Your language preference
- Your time format preference (12h or 24h)
- Your theme preference (dark, light, or system)
- The last known fasting phase state (for transition notifications)

This data never leaves your device. It is not accessible to the developer or any third party.

## No Network Transmission

Intermittent Fasting does not make any network requests to external servers. The application:

- Does not use analytics or crash reporting
- Does not include advertising SDKs
- Does not send telemetry
- Does not require an account or login

The only network access requested is to load the application itself from the Even Hub platform — this is managed by the Even Realities App and is not used to transmit personal data.

## Phone Notifications

When you grant notification permission, the application may display local system notifications on your phone to alert you of fasting phase transitions (e.g., "Fasting started"). These notifications are generated entirely on-device and are not sent to or from any server.

## Sound

The application may play a short audio beep using the Web Audio API to accompany phase transition notifications. No audio is recorded or transmitted.

## Third-Party Access

No third parties have access to your data because no data leaves your device.

## Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated as the application evolves. The latest version will always be available on the Even Hub store listing.

## Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact the developer through the Even Hub platform.
