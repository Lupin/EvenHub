## Campaign Strategy

### Positioning
"Your fast, always in sight." — Intermittent Fasting is the first dedicated fasting tracker for smart glasses. No phone check, no app switching. Glance at your G2 and know exactly where you are in your fasting cycle.

### Key Messages
1. **Glanceable** — fasting status visible on your glasses, no phone needed
2. **Beautiful** — clean timeline with Unicode progress bar, dark/light/system themes
3. **Private** — fully offline, no account, no cloud, no tracking
4. **Multilingual** — 2  languages, 12h/24h formats
5. **Smart** — phase transition notifications with phone alert + sound
