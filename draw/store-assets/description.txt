DRAW turns your G2 glasses into a pixel-art canvas.

Paint with 17 brush sets — Blocks, Triangles, Shapes, Box frames, Alphabet, Numbers, Symbols (stars, hearts, spades), Arrows, Korean Hangul, Japanese Katakana, and more. Create giant 5x5 block-letter text compositions. Everything you save goes to the gallery, where you can display it on your glasses, browse with swipe gestures, share, or export.

MODES

Draw mode — pick a brush set, choose a glyph, and paint on a resizable grid (6–28 columns, 6–7 rows). Eraser, clear, save to gallery. Push to glasses sends your canvas to the G2.

Text mode — type up to 4 characters rendered as 5x5 block letters. Toggle random glyph mode. Save stores the composition with your text as its title. Push to glasses displays it.

Gallery — all saved drawings and text. Tap for full-screen detail with prev/next arrows, position counter, and actions: Display (push to glasses), Share (export as text), Rename, Delete. Export All dumps the entire gallery as one text block. Empty gallery shows a "reload sample" link to restore the Vasarely-inspired default drawing.

GLASSES GESTURES

Swipe — enter gallery mode and browse all drawings.
Tap — next drawing.
Double-tap — return to your last pushed composition.
Double-tap again — exit.

Each drawing shows its name and position. Drawing name and a 28x7 grid of curated glyphs fill the startup screen.
