Help

DRAW turns your G2 glasses into a pixel-art canvas. Paint with block and shape glyphs, compose giant letters, and browse your creations with gestures.

Draw Mode

Pick a brush set (11 available: Blocks, Triangles, Shapes, Box sets, Mix) and a glyph, then paint on the grid with your finger. Use ⌫ Eraser to remove cells or Clear to start over. Save stores your drawing in the gallery. Push to glasses sends the current canvas to your G2.

Text Mode

Type up to 4 characters (default: DRAW). They are rendered as 5×5 block letters using the selected glyphs. Toggle Random to pick a random glyph for each pixel. Save stores the composition with the text as its title. Push to glasses sends it to your G2.

Gallery

All saved drawings and text compositions. Tap an item to see it full-size with prev/next arrows (◀ ▶), position counter, and actions: Display (push to glasses), ↗ Share (export as text via share sheet or clipboard), ✎ Rename, ✕ Delete. Export All at the top dumps the entire gallery as one text block. When empty, a reload sample link restores the Vasarely-inspired default drawing.

On the glasses

Swipe up or down to enter gallery mode and browse all your saved drawings. Tap to advance to the next one. Double-tap to return to your last pushed draw or text composition. Double-tap again to exit the app.

Grid & Theme

Use C/R +/- buttons to resize the canvas (6–28 columns, 6–7 rows). The theme selector (☀️) switches between System, Dark, and Light modes using the Even OS design palette.
